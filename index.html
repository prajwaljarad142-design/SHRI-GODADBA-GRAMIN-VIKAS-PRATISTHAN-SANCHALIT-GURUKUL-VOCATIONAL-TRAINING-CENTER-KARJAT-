About Us

Skill Development for a Stronger Future

Godadba Gram Vikas Prathisthan is a vocational training centre in Karjat focused on skill development, practical learning, and better employment opportunities for students.

We provide practical training for the construction field through experienced trainers, site-related knowledge, and career-oriented support designed to prepare students for real work environments.

Mission

Empower students with job-ready skills in construction and site supervision.

Focus

Affordable vocational education, practical learning, and employment readiness.

Approach

Hands-on training, theory support, documentation guidance, and student mentoring.

Career Opportunities

Career Opportunities After Course

Students become eligible to apply for opportunities after successful completion.

After successful completion of the Construction Supervisor Course, students become eligible to apply for various government and private sector opportunities.

Government Job Opportunities

  • Junior Engineer (JE)
  • Construction Supervisor (ZP / PWD related work)
  • Site Inspector
  • Technical Assistant

Note: Eligibility for government jobs like Junior Engineer depends on official recruitment criteria and exams.

Private Sector Opportunities

  • Site Supervisor
  • Construction Manager Assistant
  • Contractor Assistant
  • Real Estate Project Supervisor
